Mahdieh Heidaripour

Computer Science PhD student at Augusta University

I am a computer science PhD student at Augusta University, working as a Graduate Research Assistant at the SysSec Lab under the supervision of Dr. Hoda Maleki. My primary research areas are Applied Cryptography and Searchable Encryption, with a focus on secure data outsourcing, multi-dimensional databases, and privacy-preserving query processing. I am currently working on developing secure solutions for range queries, aggregation queries, and KNN queries on encrypted databases outsourced to untrusted cloud servers.
